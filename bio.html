Biography

Cleave Hay has been working with boards of trustees across NZ for the past 13 years, with a particular passion for boards focussing on their primary role of ensuring every student at their school is given the best possible education and opportunities to reach their individual highest level of achievement. He commenced in school governance in 2000 as a school trustee and chair in Timaru but now resides in Dunedin. He has also been a statutory appointee in a number of schools as a Specialist Advisor, Limited Statutory Manager and Commissioner.
